About

We are you! Friends with a passion for all things “yet to come” and “off the beaten track”. All honest. All Authentic. We are creating a movement to bring creators even closer to their supporters.


Creators are some of the most inspirational people on the planet, we want to make sure that they and their supporters can continue to make the world a more colourful place.

Product

inToken's are designed to be an additional way for Content Creators and their supporters to engage. Currently we see various ‘Pay for exclusive content’ ‘Pay for access to chat-rooms’ and even ‘Pay for virtual stickers’. We want to bring something a little more meaningful to the relationship.


Something that shows your continued support to your Creators, something that helps Creators maintain income levels and something that rewards supports for being there for the journey.


While we’ve all had a similar experience to this point, the origin of our journeys has given us all a slightly different perspective, differing tastes in Fashion, Music and Film. All Honest. All Authentic.
