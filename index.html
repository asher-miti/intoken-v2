About

inToken is the place for content creators and fans to connect and interact in a way that is truly mutually beneficial. inToken uses blockchain technology and crypto assets to bring creators and fans together in a way that is going to enhance their relationship even further.


At inToken, we are just like you…fans of great creators. The way we consume and share, music, comedy, movies, art has evolved. The faces on our screens have evolved. As a generation we have embraced Content creators being focal points for culture.


At inToken, we’ve embraced taking this idea one step further...

Product

inToken has created a more meaningful relationship between creators and their fans.


Creators are given a personalised inToken and now fans can invest in their favourite creators in the same way people invest in companies on the stock market.


With inToken, fans are now able to support the growth of creators and benefit from their success as they continue to produce more content and grow.
